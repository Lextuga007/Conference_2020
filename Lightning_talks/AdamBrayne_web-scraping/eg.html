Obituaries

Jeremy Puddle

BMJ 2020; 371 doi: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmj.m4185 (Published 30 October 2020) Cite this as: BMJ 2020;371:m4185
  • Article
  • Related content
  • Metrics
  • Responses
  • Peer review
  1. Fiona Crosbie

Former general practitioner Puddle (b 1 932; q Birmingham 1 956; PhD), died from a heart attack on 8 March 2010. After house officer posts in Luton Jeremy undertook a PhD in the effect of antiplatelet medicines on stroke, before changing heart aged 33 to general practice. He loved golf, autumnal walks and his cocker spaniel Terry. He leaves behind two children, one gastroenterologist and one astronaut, as well as his ex-wife Denise, from whom he is estranged.
